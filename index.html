What is the problem?

In this age of technological miracles, the elderly still continue to face the issue of verbal impairment, struggling to communicate with their caretakers due to the inability to easily speak. Many apps are currently working to resolve this issue, but few are easy for the elderly to use. This is especially relevant in the pandemic where people have to wear masks or use video conferencing apps to talk to each other, making communication for the elderly even harder.

Our solution

Our solution is to create a touch-activated app that speaks and listens for the elderly, delivering their requests directly to care workers. By using code, we can restore the voice of the elderly who struggle with technology, and enhance the acuity of elderly people’s senses.
